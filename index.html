Diseases Information

Below you'll find information about common diseases, their symptoms, and recommended treatments. Always consult with a healthcare professional for accurate diagnosis and treatment.

Hypertension

Symptoms: Often no symptoms, but can include headaches, shortness of breath, nosebleeds

Treatment: Lifestyle changes, medication, regular monitoring

Diabetes

Symptoms: Increased thirst, frequent urination, hunger, fatigue, blurred vision

Treatment: Insulin therapy, oral medications, diet management, exercise

Asthma

Symptoms: Shortness of breath, chest tightness, wheezing, coughing

Treatment: Inhalers, avoiding triggers, long-term control medications

Arthritis

Symptoms: Joint pain, stiffness, swelling, decreased range of motion

Treatment: Pain relievers, anti-inflammatory drugs, physical therapy
